BRIEF SYSTEM DESCRIPTION

The Telemetry equipment condition monitoring system ensures the monitoring of equipment, which in turn allows for controlling the integrity and operability of all equipment. It prevents equipment downtime, leading to increased productivity and, consequently, the company's profit.

SOFTWARE PURPOSE:

  • providing a list of all equipment with search and categorization capabilities by type;
  • providing dynamically updated information on each piece of equipment;
  • providing information on errors occurring during equipment operation;
  • providing recommendations for eliminating errors that have occurred on the equipment.
